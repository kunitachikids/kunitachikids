Educational Policies

Our vision

  1. Cultivate self-motivated and self-disciplined students by praising them for their efforts in every day life and study.

  2. Foster students' passion and interest toward academic study and other hobbies through an academic atmosphere that creates true joy and desire for learning without forcing or pressuring students to study.

  3. Cultivate students' spirit of risk-taking through encouraging them not to be afraid of failures. Failure is the mother of success, so we desire to help students develop resilience through perseverance.

  4. Cultivate students' respect towards their parents, teachers, classmates and others.

  5. Cultivate the values of sharing, caring, loving and helping each other.

  6. Cultivate globally minded students who collaborate and compete with others respectfully. Educate students about their own culture, while developing curiosity and respect for other cultures.

  7. Foster students' responsibility toward their behavior through encouraging them to apologize when they make mistakes, as well as learning a lesson from their misbehavior to improve in the future.
